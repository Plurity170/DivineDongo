Week 1: Digital Basics

Week 1 Goal

By the end of Week 1, you should feel confident using your phone for learning, not just entertainment. You will understand how the internet works and how to use free tools safely.

Lesson 1: Using Your Phone for Learning

  • Your phone is a learning tool, not only for scrolling
  • Learning requires focus and intention
  • Short daily practice is better than rushing

Lesson 2: Understanding the Internet

  • Websites are pages of information
  • Links take you from one page to another
  • Data and Wi-Fi connect your phone to the internet

Lesson 3: Searching Properly

  • Use clear and simple search words
  • Do not trust the first result blindly
  • Look for useful and trusted information

Lesson 4: Staying Safe Online

  • Never share passwords or one-time codes
  • Avoid "get rich quick" promises
  • Real skills take time to build

Practical Tasks

  • Search for useful information using your browser
  • Open a link and return back safely
  • Save a useful website
  • Create simple digital notes on your phone
